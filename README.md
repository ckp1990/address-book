# Address Book App - User Guide

Welcome to your new Address Book! This is a secure, private place to store all your contacts.

## How to Get Started

### 1. Open the App
Launch the application URL in your web browser. You will be greeted by a **Log In** screen.

### 2. Create an Account
1. Click the **"Create an account"** button at the bottom of the login box.
2. Enter your **Email Address** and create a **Password**.
3. Click "Sign Up".
4. **Important**: Go to your email inbox. You will receive a confirmation link from "Supabase". Click that link to activate your account.

### 3. Adding a Contact
Once logged in:
1. Click the blue **"New Contact"** button at the top right.
2. Fill in the details (Name, Phone number, etc.).
3. Click **"Add Contact"**.
   - *Tip: If you leave the "Avatar URL" blank, we will automatically create a colorful avatar for them!*

### 4. Searching
Use the search bar at the top left to instantly find people by name, email, or phone number as you type.

### 5. Managing Contacts
- **Edit**: Click the **Pencil icon** on any contact card to update their details.
- **Delete**: Click the **Trash icon** to remove a contact permanently.
- **Log Out**: Click the **Exit icon** at the top right when you are done.

---
**Need Help?**
If you see a "Demo Mode" warning at the top, it means the database is not connected yet. Please contact the administrator to fix the database keys.
